Polaris: Your Compass to Success

Just like the North Star guides travelers through the darkness, Polaris is your compass to discovering your direction. Many students are still deciding where they want to go and what they want to do, and that uncertainty is part of the journey. This page helps students explore their strengths, choose a direction, and build confidence in the next steps.

The quiz is designed to help you learn more about yourself and your natural skills. When you understand your own abilities better, you can find the opportunities that fit you best and make smarter choices for school, learning, and career growth.

Learn Your Skills. Unlock Your Opportunities.

After you complete the quiz, your personalized journey awaits. Discover which type of learner and thinker you are through our four unique result profiles, then use that insight to choose the right next step.

Explore the Opportunities section to see which activities, programs, and skill-building paths match your profile. Use the Skills Hub to discover courses, tools, and resources that sharpen your strengths and open new doors.

Ready to begin? Take the quiz, find your Polaris, and let the results guide you to the journey that fits your talents and goals.

Mission

Guiding youth towards financial capabilities with skills, values & character. We believe every student has a unique set of strengths waiting to be discovered. Polaris exists to make that discovery simple, personal, and actionable.
